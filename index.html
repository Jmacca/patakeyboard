Here's how it works


  1. Use your keyboard to check out the sounds - there is 26 of them in each of the six sets.

  2. Use Space to change the current set. You know it works if the background changes

  3. Fill the top panel with the sounds you like - click on their representation on the digital keyboard

  4. Should you have any doubts about your sequence choice, use the arrows to navigate and change your mistakes. Remember "To err is human."
  5. Enjoy your loop!

  6. To add list : manipulating loop interval, keyDown event trigger instead of only sound playing, mp3 compiling+export

  7. Done : start + stop button, current column indicator

  8. Ideas: OnScreen arrows to manipulate the volume / playback sound of each row?
