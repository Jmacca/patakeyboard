Here's how it works


  1. Use your keyboard to check out the sounds - there is 26 of them in each of the six sets.

  2. Use Space to change the current set. You know it works if the background changes

  3. Fill the top panel with the sounds you like - click on their representation on the digital keyboard. Should you want to get rid of a sound simply click on the digital "Space"

  4. Should you have any doubts about your sequence choice, use the arrows to navigate and change your mistakes. Remember "To err is human."
  5. Enjoy your loop!
